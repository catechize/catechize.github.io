Support

Catechize for iPhone and iPad

Contact us

Questions, problems, or feedback about Catechize? Email us at info@codetiger.co and we'll get back to you — usually within a day or two. When reporting a problem, it helps to include your device model, iOS version, and what you were doing when it happened.

Common questions

Purchases not showing? Open the Catechize+ screen and tap “Restore Purchases”. Managing or canceling a subscription: subscriptions are handled by Apple — go to Settings > Apple Account > Subscriptions on your device. Refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Privacy

Catechize has no accounts and collects no personal information. Read the full Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
